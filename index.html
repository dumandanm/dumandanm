About Me

I am Miguel Dumandan Jr, 22 years of age. I am from Libertad Norte, Sagbayan, Bohol. I have 4 siblings and I'm the youngest and the most gwapo of all.
I love to play billiards and I've been into big tournaments around bohol.
I also love music and I'm a member of a band called Pandemix band. The name is the combination of "pandemic" and "mix" since the band was founded in the middle of the pandemic last 2021. We are 6 in the band and I am the rhythm and also one of the vocals of the band. We've been performing in my places in Bohol like Panglao, Tagbilaran, Tubigon, Calape and many other places.
I am also a small engine mechanic and we have a family business which is a motor shop in Buenavista, Bohol.
